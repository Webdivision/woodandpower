The Art of the Craft

From the forest floor to your workspace

Every piece we craft starts with a journey into the Appalachian woodlands of Virginia to select timber with singular grain patterns and structural integrity.

We respect the natural history of our wood. Using a combination of traditional hand-planing and modern slab flattening, we preserve the organic raw edge of the slab. We then pair this organic beauty with robust, custom-welded steel bases and hand-rubbed oil finishes.

But we don't stop at the surface. Underneath the timber lies hidden engineering: router-channeled power cords, flush steel grommets, and smart charging ports designed to keep your space uncluttered and powered.

— Todd Blackmon, Master Craftsman

Privacy Policy

At Wood & Power, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle information submitted through our website.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when drafting a custom commission through our Interactive Quote Configurator. This includes your name, email address, custom furniture specifications, leg choices, tech options, dimensions, and special design instructions.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you submit is used solely to evaluate your custom request, cross-reference it against our raw wood slab inventory at our Virginia workshop, and correspond with you regarding your quote. If checked, we may occasionally email you about new timber arrivals or workshop updates.

3. Data Protection and Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. All submissions are stored securely. We may store data locally on your device (using browser LocalStorage) so that you can view your draft commission log at any time.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to have your commission drafts removed, please contact us at info@woodandpower.com.

Craft Agreement

Terms of Commission

By submitting a commission draft and placing a deposit with Wood & Power LLC, you agree to the following craftsman terms and guidelines:

1. Wood Characteristics & Variations

We work exclusively with solid hardwood timber slabs. Wood is an organic material that reacts to temperature and humidity. Natural features such as growth rings, live bark edges, grain swirls, small cracks (checks), and knots are characteristic of raw timber and are celebrated. These natural markings are not considered defects. Wood will expand, contract, and alter color over time as it adjusts to your home environment.

2. Commission Deposits & Lead Times

All custom pieces require a 50% non-refundable deposit before we begin slab preparation, welding, or assembly. This deposit covers timber sourcing and shop materials. Standard workshop lead times are estimated at 6 to 12 weeks, depending on design complexity. The remaining 50% balance must be paid upon project completion, prior to pickup or delivery.

3. Technology & Power Safety

Our integrated Qi wireless chargers and custom outlets are built with certified, high-grade electronics recessed and routed through solid timber. We conduct comprehensive electrical and thermal testing before delivery. However, clients must ensure all connected cables are plugged into surge-protected outlets, and the desk or table must not be exposed to water or moisture.

4. Freight, Delivery & Pickup

Clients are welcome to schedule a pickup by appointment at our Blue Ridge, Virginia workshop. If freight shipping is required, shipping costs are the sole responsibility of the client and will be billed separately. We ensure all slabs are crated with shock-absorbing foam to prevent transport damage.

Engineering the Future

Seamless Power Integration

Flush Qi Wireless Charging

We machine a precise cavity beneath the top surface of the wood. The wireless transmitter charges devices directly through up to 8mm of solid hardwood, marked by a beautiful flush steel or scorched-wood inlay.

Internal Routing & Cable Runs

Say goodbye to tangled wires. Our table slabs and desks feature back-channeled cable paths and legs with integrated hollow cores to route power lines cleanly down to outlet sources.

Custom Installed Outlets

Heavy duty outlets can be integrated flush to the edge or surface of your desk or table. Hand-crafted plates ensure the power options match the aesthetic of raw lumber.
